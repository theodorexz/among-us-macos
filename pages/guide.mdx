# Quick Recap (requirements to continue properly)

- Installed **Rosetta 2**
- Installed **Homebrew**
- Installed **Sikarugir** using Homebrew.
*If you have not installed each of these dependencies, please refer to the [Dependencies page](./dependencies.mdx)*


## How to download

Now that we have all of our Dependencies installed, we can start to configure our instance. First, open the Sikarugir Creator app (via Spotlight) and click on the blue Download Template 1.x.xx button. This is your wrapper.

Next, we have to chose our engine. Sikarugir supports multiple engines, but for this guide, we will be using the **WS12WineSikarugir10.0_4**. Once you have selected the engine, click on the blue **Create** button, you will be prompted to select a location and a name for your empty app. I reccomend the name Steam After you save your configuration Sikarugir will take a while to load and create your app. Once this ~1 minute process is complete, you will recieve a prompt from Sikarugir Creator that your app has been created. Now choose to launch the app, and finally we're in the last couple of steps to get Steam running on our Mac system.

Once the app has launched, confirm you're window looks like the screenshot below.
