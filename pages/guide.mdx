# Quick Recap (requirements to continue properly)

- Installed [**Rosetta 2**]()
- Installed **Homebrew**
- Installed **Sikarugir** using Homebrew.
*If you have not installed each of these dependencies, please refer to the [Dependencies page](./dependencies.mdx)*


## How to download

Now that we have all of our Dependencies installed, we can start to configure our instance. First, open the Sikarugir Creator app (via Spotlight) and click on the blue Download Template 1.x.xx button. This is your wrapper.

Next, we have to chose our engine. Sikarugir supports multiple engines, but for this guide, we will be using the **WS12WineSikarugir10.0_4**. Once you have selected the engine, click on the blue **Create** button. After it's done creating, you will be prompted to select a location and a name for your empty app. After you save your configuration Sikarugir will take a while to load and create your app. Once it is done, 

> *** I myself have had issues with Sikarugir taking immense times for creating the app, what I recommend is to let it run for a while, and if it takes too long, you can force quit the app and try again. If it still doesn't work, you can try to restart your Mac and try again. Lastly, visit [FAQ & Commn Issues](./faq)
