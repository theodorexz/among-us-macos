# FAQ & Common Issues

- **Q: Why do kittens sleep so much?**
  A: Kittens sleep a lot because they are growing rapidly, and sleep is essential for their development. It helps their bodies and brains to grow and develop properly.

- **Q: Why are kittens so cute?**
  A: Kittens are cute because they have big eyes, small noses, and soft fur, which trigger a nurturing response in humans.

- **Q: Is it true that ginger kittens/cats are more enegetic than other color cats.?**
  A: There is no scientific evidence to support the idea that ginger cats are more energetic than cats of other colors. A cat's energy level is more influenced by its breed, age, and individual personality rather than its coat color.

- **Q: Why are kittens so crazy all the time?**
  A: Kittens are naturally energetic and curious, which can make them seem "crazy." They are exploring their environment, learning new skills, and practicing hunting behaviors, which is all part of their development.
